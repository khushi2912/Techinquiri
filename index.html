    Growth for all Peer-peer learning Skill Development Learning by doing Mentoring

    Techinquiri provides a platform for college going students to contribute back to society along with improving their Public Speaking, People Management and Individual Learning by allowing them to learn tech topics in order to teach school students of their cities and contributing to their growth along-with improving themselves.

    We are a Student-Centered Organization working to change the way education is perceived and practiced in India, by teaching students Tech topics in a way we desired someone would have taught us. We encourage Teaching and learning on both ends and hence college students practice teaching school students of their hometown, enabling them with a special connect to each other leading to learner friendly environment in Tech-Workshops.

    Our Vision

    We envision to make learning more meaningful.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to enable students, learn and teach beyond academics, to change the way education is perceived and practiced in India.

    Our Plan

    We aim to spread the impact of our mission to major tier-2 and tier-3 cities where we can reach out to inquisitive students and bring an impactful change in their learning approach.
